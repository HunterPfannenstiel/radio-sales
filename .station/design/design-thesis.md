## On Air

**On Air** is the operating metaphor. In every recording studio, that red light means *it counts now* — what you say is live, the numbers are real, performance is being measured. RadioSales borrows that energy and turns it into a sales dashboard.

The visual language follows from the metaphor. The sidebar is the control room: dark charcoal, always present, instrument-grade. The main canvas is warm off-white — a stage under good lighting. On Air red (`--color-accent-primary`) is the signal color: it marks what's live, what's critical, what's leading.

Barlow Condensed runs the typography. It's built for scoreboards and marquees — numbers at hero size feel like a final score, not a report. The vibe is a salesperson who knows their numbers and isn't shy about them: assertive, readable at a glance, memorable for the right reasons.

The VU meter progress bars make the radio metaphor tangible. Twenty segments filling in isn't just a style choice — it's the most specific thing in the UI, the detail that says *this product knows what world it lives in.*
