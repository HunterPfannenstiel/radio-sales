# Details

## Philosophy

Surface all data without labels. Color is the only status indicator — it draws the manager's eye without making judgments for them. The manager interprets; the dashboard informs.

## Surfaced Columns

- Rep name
- Sold $ and Sold % to goal
- Projected $ and Projected % to goal
- Pace — expected sold $ vs actual sold $ at this point in the month
- Row color indicating risk state

## Risk State

Derived from projected % to goal. Three states, indicated by row color only:

- Green: projected 90%+ of goal
- Yellow: projected 70–89% of goal
- Red: projected below 70% of goal

No label is displayed. The projection number carries the meaning; color signals where to look.

## Pace

Based on a straight-line distribution of goal across the month. Expected sold $ at the current day = (days elapsed / days in month) × monthly goal. Surfaced as two numbers — expected and actual — with color applied to indicate direction.
