# Project Thesis

Simplicity is the north star
Minimal tension between intent and execution — if I want to log a call for this business I should know exactly where to go with minimal clicks
Actionable data over record keeping — record keeping is the means, actionable insight is the end
Every piece of data shown should demand a response. If it doesn't, it doesn't belong on screen.
NOT a CRM — every other tool makes reps maintain records. This one makes records work for the rep.
Goals are the north star for the rep, every single thing a rep does in the app revolves around goals
