# Jordan, Radio Sales Rep

Carries 40–60 accounts. Spends the day making calls from their car or desk. Knows their book from memory but forgets what they promised to do next. Doesn't want to manage software — wants to make calls and close business.

**Uses this page to answer one question:** *Who do I call today?*

**Decision lens:** If a feature helps Jordan answer that question faster or act on it with fewer taps, it belongs. If it adds something to manage, track, or configure, it doesn't.

---

## Scenario: Updating Stage & Next Step

Jordan just got off a call. Something changed — the GM agreed to a meeting, the deal stalled, a voicemail finally got returned. Jordan opens the business to make it accurate: move the stage, rewrite the next step. One account, one update, done.

They'd also do this without a triggering call — a quick email reply, a chance run-in, or a 10-minute sweep to clean up a stale book. They may have already logged the call via Quick Log but want to reword the next step they picked from the list.

**Decision lens:** Does this help Jordan make one fast, accurate update and move on? Anything that adds steps, asks for more context than needed, or feels like data entry works against it.
