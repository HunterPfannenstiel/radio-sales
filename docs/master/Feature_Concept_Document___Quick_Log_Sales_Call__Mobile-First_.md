A rep needs a fast, mobile-first way to record real sales activity in the moment so the dashboard can stay accurate and the rep can stay focused on productive work. This feature is the quick log flow for a sales call. It is designed to be completed in seconds on a phone browser, and it captures just enough structured information to keep opportunities progressing and to support coaching and accountability.

A “call” in this system is a real sales call, whether it happens in person, by phone, or in a virtual meeting. When a rep logs a call, the only required fields are the business name, the current stage in the sales process, and a “what’s next” selection. For v1, a rep can create a new business on the fly by entering only the business name. The rep can also log multiple interactions for the same business over time; each interaction is a separate log entry tied to that business.

The rep selects the stage from the defined sales process: approach, uncover, present, close, and service/upsell. The rep also selects a “what’s next” from a predefined list so that every logged interaction results in a clear next action that keeps the sale progressing. “What’s next” is structured (selected from a list) rather than free-text, to support consistent coaching and reporting.

The quick log supports optional “ask” capture when appropriate, but it never blocks submission. Even if the rep selects Present or Close, the rep is not required to enter an ask amount; the system may nudge the rep to capture an ask, but the rep can still submit the call log without it. When the rep does enter an ask, they can enter the budget amount and the term length. Term length is entered as a number plus a selection of weeks or months, and the system standardizes to months using a simple conversion of 4 weeks per month. The rep also selects how they feel about the sale using a confidence level of IN, SURE, EXPECT, or HOPE. The rep can mark the outcome of that specific interaction/ask as pending, yes, or no. Outcome applies to that specific interaction rather than overwriting the status of the entire business, since there can be multiple asks and interactions with the same business throughout the year.

This feature intentionally prioritizes speed and adoption over perfect data hygiene in v1. Duplicate business names are allowed rather than strictly prevented, and the minimum information required to log activity is kept small. The goal is to build the daily habit of logging focused sales activity and next steps, so the rep and manager can use the data as shared accountability and coaching truth.
