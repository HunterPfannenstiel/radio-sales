This feature defines the passive in-app flags that highlight when a rep (or a manager viewing a rep) is behind pace on the behaviors and outcomes that drive goal attainment. These are not proactive notifications. They appear as on-screen indicators when the user opens the dashboard.

Delivery and behavior: Flags are in-app only and passive. There are no push notifications, texts, or emails in v1. The purpose is to create an immediate “what do I need to do today?” moment when the app is opened.

Flag set (v1):

- Calls pace flag: indicates whether the rep is behind or on/above pace for calls week-to-date versus the manager-set weekly call target (paced Monday–Friday).

- Asks pace flag: indicates whether the rep is behind or on/above pace for asks week-to-date versus the manager-set weekly ask target (paced Monday–Friday). An “ask” is any interaction where an ask amount is entered.

- Money pace flag: indicates whether the rep is behind or on/above pace for sold+projected month-to-date versus the monthly goal.

Audience and placement: These flags are visible to both reps and managers. Reps see them on their own dashboard. Managers see them on the rep list and in rep drill-down views.

Color logic: Flags use a simple binary color system.

- Green: on pace or above pace.

- Red: behind pace.

The binary approach is intentional for v1 to keep interpretation fast, reduce debate about thresholds, and reinforce consistent daily coaching behavior.
