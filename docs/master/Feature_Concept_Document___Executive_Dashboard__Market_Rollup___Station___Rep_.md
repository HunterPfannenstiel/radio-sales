This feature defines the executive-level dashboard experience for leadership users who need a fast, high-level view of goal attainment across the organization, with the ability to drill down to understand which areas are driving performance.

Default view and hierarchy: The executive dashboard defaults to a property-first rollup. In v1, the primary “property” dimension is Market. Executives see a list of markets and can drill down into the stations within a selected market. From station level, executives can further drill down to rep-level performance.

Metrics shown (exec level): The executive view is intentionally money-focused to stay executive-friendly and avoid clutter. For each market, the dashboard shows Sold percent to goal and Projected versus goal. Activity pacing (calls/asks) is not shown at the executive market rollup level in v1.

Drill-down intent: The drill-down path (Market → Station → Rep) is designed to help leadership quickly identify where performance is strong or weak, then locate the underlying station(s) and rep(s) contributing to that outcome. This supports executive visibility and accountability without requiring executives to manage day-to-day activity coaching.

Role and access: Executives have permission to view rep-level performance through drill-down. This includes the same core money performance indicators used throughout the system (Sold % to goal and Projected vs goal), consistent with the definitions established in the Sold/Projected feature.
