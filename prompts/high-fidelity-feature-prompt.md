## Session Goal
- A roadmap to take a newly implemented feature from low-fidelity to high-fidelity

## Process
1. Examine the low-fidelity artifact and understand its purpose
2. Research relevant products, provided by the user, to come up with what a high-fidelity UI/UX version of this feature would entail.
    - Pull inspiration from these products but also suggest things we could make different
3. Examine the application-wide design standards for any extra knowledge you may need
4. Present your findings to the user and include the rationale for every single decision made. If there are any discrepancies between the design standards and your findings also present those to the user.

## What not to include
- Don't include any fine-tuning such as loading states, error handling, etc... This is purely a presentational pursuit
